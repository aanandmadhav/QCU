About JAS-ANZ
JAS-ANZ is the government-appointed accreditation

body for Australia and New Zealand responsible for providing accreditation of conformity assessment bodies (CABs) in the fields of certification and inspection. Accreditation by JAS-ANZ demonstrates the competence and independence of these CABs.

Accredited CABs like Quest Certification can then provide certification and inspection services to organizations.
